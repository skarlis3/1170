Generative AI Policy

Because ENGL 1170 is a co-requisite support class paired with ENGL 1181, the Generative AI policy for both classes is the same. Remember that at the end of the day, you are a better writer than AI. So, write your messy first drafts, do your own thinking, and only use GenAI in ways that support your learning instead of replace it.

See the ENGL 1181 Generative AI Policy for the full policy for this class.

If you want to see how I use (and won't use) AI from the instructor side of things, see the Instructor GenAI Use page on the ENGL 1181 site.

AI tools were also used in the design and coding of this site and may have been used for proofreading, brainstorming, or refining course content.
